Can awareness about 'the effects of weather on flight experirence' help passengers make better choices?

An exploration for Domestic Travel - Civil Aviation, India.

One of the major causes of the disrupted on-time performance of India's domestic flights is weather. Changing trends in parameters contributing to weather especially temperature, rainfall and even shift in the seasons has a direct effect on the flight cancellations, delays causing inconvenience to passengers. This visualization analayzes the monthly cancellations, complaints for prominent airline carriers of scheduled domestic air travel in India along with monthly temperature and rainfall patterns for last 5 years 2014-18
